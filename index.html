Precipitation in Oregon Cities 2017 - 2021

Let it Rain!

The summer of 2021 will be remebered by the heat dome which hovered over the pacific northwest and brought some of the highest temps ever recorded in the region. The same summer was marked by massive wildfires whose smoke blanketed much of the US. In Oregon, these anomalus events were preceeded by a percieved exceptionally dry year.

This map explores the month by month preciptation totals in Oregon cities from January 2017 to December 2021. Explore the changes from the statewide view or search a city or apply a filter to observe from a more targeted perspective.
